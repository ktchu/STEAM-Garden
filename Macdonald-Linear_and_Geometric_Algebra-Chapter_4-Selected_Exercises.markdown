### 4.1.

By definition,
$ \mathbf{u} \cdot \mathbf{v} = |\mathbf{u}| |\mathbf{v}| \cos \theta $,
where $\theta$ is the angle between the oriented lengths. Therefore,
$\mathbf{v} \cdot \mathbf{v} = |\mathbf{v}|^2$ because $\theta = 0$ in this
case and $\cos \theta = 1$.
